Car Detailing System ERD - Draw.io Instructions

STEP 1: Go to https://app.diagrams.net/ (Draw.io)
STEP 2: Create new diagram
STEP 3: Use these elements:

ENTITIES (Use Rectangle shapes):
1. USERS
2. SERVICES  
3. BOOKINGS
4. PAYMENTS
5. REVIEWS
6. NOTIFICATIONS
7. ACTIVITY_LOGS
8. PAYMENT_LOGS

RELATIONSHIPS (Use Diamond shapes):
1. MAKES (Users → Bookings)
2. OFFERS (Services → Bookings)
3. PAYS_FOR (Bookings → Payments)
4. VERIFIES (Users → Payments)
5. WRITES (Users → Reviews)
6. CONCERNS (Services → Reviews)
7. REVIEWS (Bookings → Reviews)
8. RECEIVES (Users → Notifications)
9. LOGS (Users → Activity_Logs)
10. CREATES (Payments → Payment_Logs)

CARDINALITY NOTATION:
- Users to Bookings: 1:N
- Services to Bookings: 1:N
- Bookings to Payments: 1:N
- Users to Payments: 1:N
- Bookings to Reviews: 1:1
- Users to Reviews: 1:N
- Services to Reviews: 1:N
- Users to Notifications: 1:N
- Users to Activity_Logs: 1:N
- Payments to Payment_Logs: 1:N

FORMATTING TIPS:
1. Use yellow rectangles for entities
2. Use blue diamonds for relationships
3. Use green ovals for attributes
4. Underline primary keys
5. Use dashed underlines for foreign keys
6. Add 1, N, M symbols at line ends
7. Use connecting lines to show relationships
